Seed — Who We Are

Canopy is the Eco Club's promise to grow with the campus that grows us.

We started Canopy on a simple belief: a campus is more than its buildings — it's the shade it offers, the air it clears, and the roots it puts down for the people who pass through it. Every drive, every sapling, every Saturday morning spent with soil under our nails is a small act of keeping that promise.

"Let's Green Our Campus and Grow Together" isn't just this event's name — it's how we measure every initiative we run. Growth we can point to, shade we can stand under, and a community that shows up for it.

Root — An Initiative By

The Department of Community Medicine is leading this drive from the ground up.

Community Medicine has always worked at the point where public health meets daily life — and a greener campus is preventive care too. This department is organising and hosting the drive, bringing together faculty from across Government Omandurar Medical College for one shared morning of planting.

Dr. S. Arun Murugan, MBBS MD HOD, Community Medicine

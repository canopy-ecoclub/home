About the Club

A canopy grown by many hands

Every tree planted here starts as one small act, repeated until a campus turns green.

Canopy is the Eco Club of Government Omandurar Medical College — a community of students, faculty, and staff working to make the campus greener, cooler, and more alive. It began with a simple belief: a hospital that heals people should also help heal the ground it stands on.

The Tree Plantation Drive is Canopy's first major initiative — an open invitation to plant, together, the shade this campus will stand under for decades.

An Initiative By

Department of Community Medicine

Guided by the department that has long championed preventive care and public health, this drive extends that mission outward - from clinics and classrooms to open ground, one sapling at a time.
